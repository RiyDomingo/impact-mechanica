We've Got Answers.

How does the 3D scanning process work?

Once you join the pilot program, you'll get access to our Kintura™ mobile app. The app uses your iPhone's TrueDepth camera to guide you through a simple, quick, and accurate 3D scan of your head, creating the digital blueprint for your custom head guard.

What is the expected cost after the pilot program?

While final pricing is yet to be determined, our goal is to make personalized protection accessible. Pilot program participants will receive an exclusive discount on the final product. Sign up to stay informed about launch pricing.

Is the head guard approved for my league?

The Valkyrie Head Guard V1 is engineered to meet and exceed World Rugby Regulation 12 performance and safety standards. This makes it eligible for play at all levels of the game, from youth leagues to professional matches.
